The Bride

Ashley Federwisch

Ashley is a proud Nevada native - she was born and raised in Las Vegas and attended University of Nevada Reno, where she met Patrick through mutual friends. Her mom knew that it was true love when she told her that her "friend" Patrick had brought her toilet paper when she was sick. They began dating and she taught him everything he knows about cooking burgers. After completing her degree and starting out her career as a lab analyst for clinical trials, she agreed that it was best for the pair to relocate back to Vegas to be closer to friends and family.
